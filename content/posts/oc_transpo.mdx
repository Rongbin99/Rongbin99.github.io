## Older Than You Think

For context, the oldest bus still operational in the [TTC's](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toronto_Transit_Commission) active bus fleet are the [2007 Orion VII NG (Next Gen) HEVs (Hybrid)](https://cptdb.ca/wiki/index.php/Toronto_Transit_Commission_1200-1423#Details). The [New Flyer Industries (NFI) D40i](https://cptdb.ca/wiki/index.php/Ottawa-Carleton_Regional_Transit_Commission_4203-4273#Details) in OC Transpo's bus fleet are from 2004. That makes these NFI D40i's older than me and 3 years older then the Orions, of which OC Transpo [retired](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/ottawa/ottawa-oc-transpo-bus-sale-1.5331534) them [all in 2020](https://cptdb.ca/wiki/index.php/Ottawa-Carleton_Regional_Transit_Commission#Retired_bus_roster).
